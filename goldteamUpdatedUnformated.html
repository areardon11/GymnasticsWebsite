Gold Team

Gold Team is our competitive team that represents Cal Gymnastics Club at regional and national competitions. The great thing about it is that it is open to everyone at all skill levels, even if you've never done gymnastics before. All you need is the desire to learn gymnastics, make friends and have fun with our flipping family.

Practice Times

Monday–Friday, 8:30–11:30p
Weekends by announcement

Mondays: competitive class only
Tuesdays–Fridays: basics class or free practice*
Weekends: free practice

* We do not offer an open gym format. All practice must be exclusively artistic gymnastics.

About the Cal Rec Club Membership

Cal Recreational Sports Club membership (often called the RSF membership after the Recreational Sports Facility) gives you access to UC Berkeley's recreational facilities, drop-in classes, sport teams and clubs.

Membership for students is $10 per semester with lots of options for associated students, faculty and staff, alumni and the general community.

To learn more about membership types, please visit Rec Sports' membership page.

  1. Before practice

    • Cal Rec Club (RSF) Membership
      various options available
    • Gold Team Dues
      $85 per semester ($55 Summer) or $20 transition*
    • Online Club Sign Up
      $0 item in online store, valid June 2014–June 2015
    • Buy the team uniform
      Uniform deposit is $160 for men, $100 for women
    Pay Gold Team dues
    in the Online Store

    We do not accept cash. All payments must be done online before practice. No exceptions will be made.

    Make sure to add the SC Gymnastics Club Sign-Up item to your cart. It will prompt you to agree to RSF waivers and code of conduct as well as fill out your emergency contact. You have to have these forms completed before practice or you will have to do it online at the gym. Again, we can make no exceptions.

    *Transition option is available to people who have already paid Blue Team dues for that semester and for the following two groups: Cal Gymnastics Club officers and Golden Bear Rec Center employees.

  2. At first practice

    • Bring proof of payment
      You can show the email confirmation on your phone or printed out.
    • Sign Waiver of Liability and Gold Team Code of Conduct
      This agrees to Cal Gymnastics Club rules and you will sign it in person. It is not the same as the online club sign up.

  3. During the academic year

    • Compete a minimum of one meet
    • Fulfill a minimum of 11 hours of fundraisers or 2.5 full Parent’s Night Out fundraisers
      If membership began at the start of a spring term, 5.5 hours of fundraising or one full PNO must be achieved.
