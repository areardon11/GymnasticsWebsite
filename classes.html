To the beginners:

A lot of members of our club have started their gymnastics journey as adults in their student years. We find it very rewarding to see anyone gain confidence with their body and themselves by learning gymnastics, especially in a setting which also cultivates friendship and teamwork.

To create the environment where you can grow together with your peers, we’ve prepared the best set of classes yet. Come and join the fun that is gymnastics!

— Volunteer coaches at Cal Gymnastics Club

Basics

Beginners Classes

1h, Monday–Friday, Everyone
This class is for Blue or Gold Team members who would like to work on their basic skills. This includes first time gymnasts and those with more experience that want to solidify their foundation before they move to advanced skills.

Intermediate Classes

1h, Monday–Friday, Everyone
This class is for Blue or Gold Team members who are ready to move beyond initial basic skills of the sport. This generally means that they are able to proficiently preform some basic A level skills on the events they are training (can do good round-offs, handstands, and front or back tucks on tumble track).

Competitive Classes

Beginners/Intermediate

2.5h, Mondays, Gold Team only
This class is for Gold Team members that want to prepare for competing on men’s or women’s event and develop initial skills. Attendees are split by gender they want to compete for (men are allowed to compete women’s events and vice versa) and we have one coach for each gender.

Advanced

2.5h, Mondays, Gold Team only
This is the most experienced class and is for Gold Team members that have at least two B level skills on the event being trained. Class is split by desired gender and each get their own coach.
